This project examines changes in socioeconomic conditions across New York City Neighborhood Tabulation Areas (NTAs) using ACS 5-year data.

Citywide Overview

At the citywide scale, patterns of change reveal uneven growth across neighborhoods.

Methodology

ACS 5-year estimates from 2010 and 2023 were joined to NTA boundaries. Change was calculated as the difference between time periods.

Neighborhood Focus

Three neighborhoods were selected to illustrate how citywide trends manifest at the local level.
