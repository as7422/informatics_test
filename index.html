digital platforms & the city

digital platforms are increasingly contributing to everyday urban experiences

social media is altering how society and the built environment are perceived

tiktok in particular positions videos that fit certain narratives and visual identities to go viral easily, altering how people engage with the city

digital platforms traditionally provide services like food delivery, navigation, ride-hailing, short-term rentals…

these platforms produce social relations, symbolic hierarchies, modes of visibility, and ways of inhabiting a place.

through these elements, they are contributing to the increasing digitization and mediatization of the city.

platform gentrification

reconfiguring traditional forms of gentrification

defining and increasing desirability of a place online can change the value of a location, providing a means of increasing gentrification.

this process is being understood as platform gentrification.

research questions

  • which neighborhoods are being impacted by platform gentrification?
  • how do digital platforms shape the symbolic economy of place?
