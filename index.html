Filtering the City

This project examines changes in socioeconomic conditions across New York City Neighborhood Tabulation Areas (NTAs) using ACS 5-year data from 2010 to 2023.

Scroll to continue.

Methodology

ACS 5-year estimates from 2010 and 2023 were joined to NTA boundaries. Change was calculated as the difference between time periods.

NTAs were selected as the unit of analysis to balance neighborhood specificity and data stability.

Citywide Overview

Citywide patterns reveal uneven change across neighborhoods, with growth concentrated in specific areas.

Neighborhood Case Studies

Three neighborhoods are examined to illustrate how citywide trends manifest at the local scale.
